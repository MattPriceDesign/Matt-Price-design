About me

Hey, thank you for checking out the site!          
If you asked me “what would you do if you didn't have to make money?” I would answer “make cool stuff with computers.” As a teenager, I spent hours every day making CGI art; my dream was to work at Pixar Animation Studios. During that time I found that combining creativity with technology can be incredibly fun.                    

A couple of years ago I talked with a UX/UI professional at Quizlet. She inspired me to take a design course and do UX work. Now I am a junior designer. I have learned the thought process, coordinated with teammates, delivered projects, and worked with clients, but I still have so much to learn. If you would like to join me on this journey and want to work with a fledgling UX designer, I would love to chat
